We know WiFi security

WiFi Routers and Smart Devices are trusted to securely transmit sensitive data. Your bank account information, home address, internet searches, and health records submitted online typically travel safely through your router to the intended recipient. However, these communications are highly susceptible to a variety of exploitation attacks if your router and home device networks are incorrectly configured for security. Apollo Devsec's goal is to implement pragmatic solutions to home cybersecurity challenges.
